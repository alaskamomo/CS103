Small Facts about Osaka Castle:

  • Osaka Castle is one of the most famous landmark of Japan
  • Building first started in 1583 and was rebuilt in 1620s (Osaka Castle - History and Facts)
  • The castle was built to become the centre of a new, unified japan (Osaka Castle (Osakajo) -Japan Guide)

What I Like About This Game

I like how every death you have in this game, it allows you to modify your loadout and utilize your deaths to advance the story along. For instance I died on the third stage but my death has allowed me to start a certain chain of events to connect two side characters to help me complete a task or call them in for assist in battle.

How is it differ from
other dungeon crawlers?

  1. Every death is used to progress the story along
  2. Allows the player to have a break midway through a run
  3. Doesn't utilize the typical dungeon crawler movement sets

Why I Chose To Talk About It

During lockdown, I was going through a very rough time with family problems and dealing with health issues. This game was sort of my comfort game during this time period. Not only did it allow me to get my mind off things but in a way get my life on track again.
